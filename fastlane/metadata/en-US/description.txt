Proiectul ShoeBox Romania este o initiativa de familie care presupune adunarea de cutii de pantofi ambalate in hartie de cadou, pentru a fi distribuite voluntar catre copiii defavorizati din Romania. In acelasi timp, reprezinta o buna modalitate de educatie pentru copiii nostri, invatandu-i sa imparta din ceea ce au, cu altii mai putini norocosi.

-------
English:
-------
Shoebox Romania Project is a family initiative started in 2006, that coordinates the donation, collection and distribution of personalized gifts, placed inside a shoebox, wrapped like a gift, at Christmas time to underprivileged children; while at the same time helping parents teach their own children about the joys of giving.
